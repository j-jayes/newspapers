Comparison: Job Title Extraction Methods
Approach How It Works Pros & Cons F1-Score
Dictionary-Based Matches ad text against a fixed list of historical job titles (HISCO).
  • Simple to implement.
  • No training data needed.
  • Misses new or misspelled titles.
 0.632
Rule-Based Uses grammatical patterns common in job ads (e.g., "job as a [noun]").
  • Flexible, handles variations.
  • No training data needed.
  • Confused by complex ads.
 0.690
NER (Machine Learning) Trains a model to recognize the "job title" entity in context.
  • Highest accuracy.
  • Can find brand new titles.
  • Requires extensive manual labeling.
 0.944
Text Generation An AI model rewrites the ad, keeping only the job titles.
  • Very high accuracy.
  • Excellent at handling OCR errors.
  • Requires extensive manual labeling.
 0.920
